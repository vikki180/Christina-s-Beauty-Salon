About Us

Christina's Beauty Salon and its staff have been a part of Columbias community for over 20 years. We value excellence in service and performance. Owner and stylist Yong first entered the business in 1995 through a shared space near the Columbia Mall. After establishing roots in the community and enjoying offering styling services, Yong decided to create her own salon. This was fully realized in 2000, when Yong opened Christina's Beauty Salon in downtown Columbia. In 2001, the salon was moved to its current location on Ash Street. Through its services, Christinas Beauty Salon offers a warm and comfortable environment for styling, coloring, and cutting hair.

Yong is both the owner and a stylist at Christinas Beauty Salon. She was encouraged to enter the beauty industry by a friend, and received training through a professional beauty school. Being a hairdresser is a rewarding experience to her, as she enjoys helping clients to become happier with their hair. Yong views clients as friends and wants to help them look their best. She works hard to make every appointment a relaxing and enjoyable experience. In addition to hairdressing skills, Yong also has a black belt in Taekwondo, a Korean martial art.



Krystina joined the team in 2015 as the manager. Krystina entered the hair industry after attending cosmetology school. She began hairdressing officially in 1995, and later went on to get her education in instructing. She often freelances as an educator. She has a Masters of Colorist in addition to her years of education and experience. In her words, “It takes all colors of the rainbow to make the world go round, and for butterflies to harmonize it.” Krystina takes time to educate clients on what products to use, which products are best for them, and why each should be used.
