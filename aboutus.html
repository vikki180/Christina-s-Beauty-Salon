About Us

Christinas Beauty Salon and its staff have been a part of Columbias community for over 20 years. We value excellence in service and performance. Owner and stylist Yong [Full Name] first entered the business in 1995 through a shared space near the Columbia Mall. After establishing roots to the community and enjoying offering styling services, Yong decided to create her own salon. This was fully realized in 2000, when Yong opened Christina’s Beauty Salon to a downtown location. In 2001, the salon was moved to its current location of Ash Street. Through its services, Christina’s Beauty Salon offers a warm and comfortable environment for styling, coloring, and cutting hair.

Yong is both the owner and a stylist of Christinas Beauty Salon. She was encouraged to enter the beauty industry by a friend, and received training through a professional beauty school. Being a hairdresser is a rewarding experience to her, as she enjoys helping clients to become happier with their hair. Yong views clients as friends to help look their best, and works hard to make every appointment a relaxing and enjoyable experience. In addition to hairdressing skills, Yong also sports a black belt in Taekwondo, a Korean martial art.



Kristina joined the Christinas Beauty Salon team in 2015, and she also works at Young Star Salons. As she manages both salons, you may find her at either location. Kristina entered the hair industry after high school when she attended cosmetology school. She began hairdressing officially in 1995, and later when on to get her education in instructing, which she uses as a freelance educator. She has a Masters of Colorist in addition to her years of education and experience. In her words, “It takes all colors of the rainbow to make the world go round, and for butterflies to harmonize it.” Kristina takes time to educate clients on what what products to use, which ones are best for them, and why each should be used. She also has interest in the field of architecture.
